Pride and Prejudice

Jane Austen

Set in rural England at the turn of the 19th century, the story centers on the Bennet family, who face financial pressure to marry off their five daughters because their estate is legally restricted to male heirs. The core narrative follows the turbulent relationship between the quick-witted, independent Elizabeth Bennet and the wealthy, reserved aristocrat Fitzwilliam Darcy. Upon their initial meeting, Darcy insults Elizabeth's appearance, triggering her deep dislike, while she perceives him as incredibly arrogant.

To Kill a Mockingbird

Harper Lee

Set in the fictional, racially segregated town of Maycomb, Alabama, during the Great Depression, the story is narrated by a young, tomboyish girl named Jean Louise "Scout" Finch. The central plot revolves around her father, Atticus Finch, a principled and compassionate defense lawyer. Atticus is appointed to defend Tom Robinson, a Black man falsely accused of raping a white woman. Despite Atticus proving Tom's clear innocence, the deeply prejudiced all-white jury convicts him. Alongside this trial, Scout, her brother Jem, and their friend Dill spend their summers fascinated by their mysterious, reclusive neighbor, Arthur "Boo" Radley

Stranger in a Strange Land on Goodreads

Robert A. Heinlein

The story follows Valentine Michael Smith, a human who was born during the first manned mission to Mars and raised entirely by native Martians after the rest of the crew died. Twenty-five years later, a second Earth expedition discovers Smith and brings him back to Earth.Because of unique legal precedents, Smith holds legal ownership of Mars and inherits an astronomical financial fortune, making him a prime target for corrupt Earth politicians. Assisted by a compassionate nurse named Jill Boardman and protected by Jubal Harshaw—an eccentric, individualistic lawyer and philosopher—Smith escapes government confinement. As he adapts to Western culture, Smith uses his extraordinary Martian-taught psychic abilities (such as telepathy and telekinesis) to found a new movement called the Church of All Worlds.
