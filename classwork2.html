ARABICA

Arabica coffee is the world's most popular coffee, praised for its smooth, sweet, and complex flavors. it's cost is $12 The most popular and widely consumed coffee worldwide, making up about 60-70% of global production. Arabica beans grow at higher altitudes and have a sweeter, more complex flavor profile with tasting notes of fruit, berries, and floral undertones. You will typically find these in specialty cafes and premium single-origin roasts.

ROBUSTA

Robusta coffee makes up most of the remaining 30-40% of global production. it's cost is $16These beans are hardier, contain nearly double the caffeine, and have a bold, earthy, and highly bitter flavor profile. Because of their strong taste and thick crema, they are highly favored for espresso blends and budget-friendly instant coffees.

LIBERICA

Liberica coffee

A rare and highly distinct species that accounts for less than 2% of the world's coffee.It's cost is $18Cultivated primarily in Southeast Asia, these unusually large beans produce a brew with a very heavy, woody, floral, and smoky flavor profile.
